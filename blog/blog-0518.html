Original Website

Before moving on to the ver.2 of this website, I think it would be a great idea to see what the ver.1 look like. I decided to revamp the website because the original version was too playful. I had my selfie as a background. I had many animation when scrolling down the page to different sections.

Yeah..I know..it was not that professional.

Therefore, I started to redo the whole website. I hope it looks somewhat professional now (other than the fact that my blog is super informal).

Why did I have a lot of animations/transitions in the first place?

Fun is the answer. Even though I have to add several line of CSS3 and HTML to do the animation, I still enjoy it because I can see it happens right then! However, now when I scroll down I got annoyed by them. That's why they are gone from ver.2.

Night,
